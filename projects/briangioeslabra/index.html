about

I am Brian Gio Eslabra, a Filipino web developer transitioning from chemical engineering. I have a fair bit of experience with usual front-end tools like HTML, CSS and JavaScript, with a sprinkle of frameworks such as Bootsrap and jQuery. I also know a bit of back-end with Node.js/Express.js. I love and enjoy coding and I hope that translates to my body of work!

Throughout my journey as a web developer, I've been constantly impressed by how much you can achieve with just a little extra technical knowledge. I'm also constantly trying to improve those technical skills not just by creating side projects but by also taking a serious academic approach to learning fundamental concepts like data structures and algorithms.

Life outside work, I enjoy spending quality time with my family. When I'm all alone, I either watch anime or listen to Kanye.
